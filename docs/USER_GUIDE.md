## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the TalentHive homepage
2. Click "Sign Up" button
3. Choose your role: **Client**, **Freelancer**, or **Admin**
4. Enter your email and create a strong password
5. Verify your email address
6. Complete your profile

### Logging In

1. Click "Login" on the homepage
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### Password Recovery

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

## Client Guide

### Posting a Project

1. **Navigate to Projects**
   - Click "Post Project" from dashboard
   - Or go to Projects → New Project

2. **Enter Project Details**
   - **Title**: Clear, descriptive project name
   - **Description**: Detailed requirements and expectations
   - **Category**: Select relevant category
   - **Skills Required**: Add required skills
   - **Budget**: Set fixed or hourly rate
   - **Timeline**: Set project deadline

3. **Add Milestones** (Optional)
   - Break project into deliverables
   - Set milestone amounts and due dates
   - Helps track progress

4. **Review and Publish**
   - Review all details
   - Click "Publish Project"
   - Project appears in marketplace

### Reviewing Proposals

1. **View Proposals**
   - Go to your project
   - Click "Proposals" tab
   - See all submitted proposals

2. **Evaluate Freelancers**
   - Check freelancer profile
   - Review portfolio and past work
   - Read reviews from other clients
   - Check skills and experience

3. **Compare Proposals**
   - Review bid amount
   - Check proposed timeline
   - Read cover letter
   - Compare with other proposals

4. **Accept or Reject**
   - Click "Accept" to hire freelancer
   - Click "Reject" to decline
   - You can accept multiple proposals

### Managing Contracts

1. **View Contract**
   - Go to Contracts section
   - Click contract to view details
   - Review terms and milestones

2. **Track Progress**
   - Monitor milestone status
   - View submitted deliverables
   - Leave feedback

3. **Approve Milestones**
   - Review freelancer's work
   - Click "Approve" if satisfied
   - Payment is released to freelancer
   - Or click "Request Changes" for revisions

4. **Dispute Resolution**
   - If issues arise, click "Create Dispute"
   - Describe the problem
   - Admin will review and mediate

### Processing Payments

1. **Payment Methods**
   - Add credit/debit card
   - Payments processed via Stripe
   - Secure and encrypted

2. **Payment Flow**
   - Funds held in escrow
   - Released after milestone approval
   - Automatic commission deduction
   - Freelancer receives payment

3. **View Payment History**
   - Go to Payments section
   - See all transactions
   - Download receipts

### Leaving Reviews

1. **After Project Completion**
   - Go to completed contract
   - Click "Leave Review"

2. **Write Review**
   - Rate freelancer (1-5 stars)
   - Write detailed feedback
   - Highlight strengths and areas for improvement

3. **Submit Review**
   - Click "Submit"
   - Review appears on freelancer's profile
   - Helps other clients make decisions

### Organizing with Teams

1. **Create Organization**
   - Go to Organizations
   - Click "Create Organization"
   - Enter organization details

2. **Invite Team Members**
   - Go to Organization Settings
   - Click "Invite Members"
   - Enter email addresses
   - Set member roles

3. **Manage Budget**
   - Set organization budget
   - Track spending
   - Approve team expenses

## Freelancer Guide

1. **Complete Profile Information**

   - Set hourly rate (if applicable)

2. **Add Skills**
   - Click "Add Skill"
   - Search and select skills
   - Prioritize top 5 skills

3. **Build Portfolio**
   - Click "Add Portfolio Item"
   - Upload project images/files
   - Write project description
   - Include links to live projects
   - Showcase your best work

4. **Get Verified**
   - Verify your email
   - Complete identity verification
   - Get blue verification badge

### Finding Projects

1. **Browse Projects**
   - Go to "Browse Projects"
   - See all available projects
   - Filter by category, budget, skills

2. **Search Projects**
   - Use search bar
   - Filter by:
     - Category
     - Budget range
     - Skills required
     - Project type
     - Timeline

3. **View Project Details**
   - Click project to view full details
   - Read requirements carefully
   - Check client reviews
   - See other proposals

4. **Save Projects**
   - Click heart icon to save
   - View saved projects later
   - Get notifications for similar projects

### Submitting Proposals

1. **Click "Submit Proposal"**
   - On project page
   - Or from saved projects

2. **Write Proposal**
   - **Bid Amount**: Your price for the project
   - **Timeline**: How long you need
   - **Cover Letter**: Why you're the right fit
     - Highlight relevant experience
     - Show understanding of requirements
     - Be professional and personable

3. **Review and Submit**
   - Check all details
   - Click "Submit Proposal"
   - Proposal sent to client

4. **Track Proposals**
   - Go to "My Proposals"
   - See proposal status
   - View client feedback
   - Update or withdraw proposals

### Managing Contracts

1. **Accept Contract**
   - Client accepts your proposal

   - Review contract terms
   - Click "Accept" to start work

2. **Submit Milestones**
   - Complete work for milestone

3. **Receive Feedback**
   - Client may request changes
   - Make revisions
   - Resubmit milestone

4. **Get Paid**
   - After client approves
   - Payment released from escrow
   - Funds transferred to your account
   - Check payment history

### Time Tracking

2. **Stop and Submit**

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is TalentHive free to use?**
A: Creating an account is free. The platform charges a commission on completed projects (default 20%).

**Q: How do I verify my account?**
A: Go to Settings → Verification. Verify your email, and optionally add phone and identity verification for a blue badge.

**Q: How long does verification take?**
A: Email verification is instant. Identity verification may take 24-48 hours.

### For Clients

**Q: How do I know if a freelancer is trustworthy?**
A: Check their profile, portfolio, reviews, and verification status. Start with smaller projects to build trust.

**Q: What if I'm not satisfied with the work?**
A: You can request changes before approving the milestone. If issues persist, create a dispute for admin mediation.

**Q: How are payments protected?**
A: Payments are held in escrow until you approve the work. This protects both you and the freelancer.

**Q: Can I hire multiple freelancers for one project?**
A: Yes, you can accept multiple proposals for different parts of your project.

### For Freelancers

**Q: How do I increase my chances of getting hired?**
A: Build a strong profile with portfolio items, get verified, maintain high ratings, and write compelling proposals.

**Q: What if a client doesn't approve my work?**
A: They can request changes. Communicate with them to understand their concerns and make revisions.

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: After the client approves a milestone, payment is released from escrow to your account within 2-3 business days.

**Q: Can I work on multiple projects simultaneously?**
A: Yes, you can manage multiple contracts at the same time.

### For Admins

**Q: How do I handle disputes?**
A: Review both sides' claims, check evidence, and make a fair decision. Communicate your decision to both parties.

**Q: How do I prevent fraud?**
A: Monitor for suspicious activity, verify user identities, review payment patterns, and take action against violators.

**Q: Can I customize commission rates?**
A: Yes, go to Settings → Commission to adjust rates for different project types or user tiers.
